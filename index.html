About

On both a professional and personal level, knowing who your putting your trust in when it comes to your business is incredibly important. Welcome to Atlantica DESIGNS, a company you can know and trust. My name is Stephanie Annabelle Hillier and I am the proud founder/owner of Atlantica DESIGNS. I am from a small island off the south coast of Newfoundland, Canada's most easterly province, which was the inspiration for the name. I grew up on the Atlantic ocean and it was some of the most special memories in my life and my goal for this company is to bring special memories, creativity and trust to our clients as they enter an important phase in their lives when starting a new business.
